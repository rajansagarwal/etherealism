Radiant Skies

The rocket takes off, Seeing the world from a different view
Breathing air so clean, Life so unseen

The Darkness Falls, Light Rises
An explosion left behind, Radiant night skies

No turning back, Just keep going
Head towards the moon, Just keep soaring

- rajan agarwal

Midnight Train

All aboard the midnight train, where nobody cares about your name
Strangers and small town city lights, momentary blurs in your night

You pay your fare but your not there, You are on the midnight train
Take no baggage, got no claim, All aboard the midnight train

Faded Levis, scuffed up boots, Midnight traveler's business suit
The only path, the railroad track, Take a seat, don't look back

- rajan agarwal

Dark Night

On a dark night, lies the dark night
alone in the pale white palace

In darkness and secure, By the secret ladder, disguised–oh, happy chance!–
In darkness and in concealment, My house being now at rest.

In the happy night, In secret, when none saw me,
Without light or guide, save that which burned in my heart.

- rajan agarwal

Olympus

Thundering down Olympus, Treads Ares
The battle master, Ashen; Piceous clad
Against midnights cloak, Returning human spirit
To the heavens, For upon drowning
Within his crimson gaze, Do we understand
That in faith— Lies divinity

- christian mystics

Autumn Fairytale

but you see,
this is an autumn fairytale
filled with stories of fallen leaves
and lifted veils
i can no longer keep
my fractures eclipsed
i watch the tree undress
and my mask too, slips

- eesha joshi
